Types of Heat Pump Systems We Service

Our expertise covers all major heat pump technologies:

Air-Source Heat Pumps

  • Traditional Heat Pumps: Standard efficiency systems for moderate climates
  • Cold Climate Heat Pumps: Specialized systems designed for Wisconsin winters
  • Variable Speed Heat Pumps: Advanced systems with precise temperature control
  • Dual-Fuel Heat Pumps: Hybrid systems combining heat pump and furnace technology
  • Mini Split Heat Pumps: Ductless heat pump systems for zoned comfort
  • Packaged Heat Pumps: Complete systems for homes without indoor space

Ground-Source (Geothermal) Heat Pumps

  • Horizontal Loop Systems: Systems with horizontal ground loops
  • Vertical Loop Systems: Deep vertical bore systems for limited space
  • Pond/Lake Systems: Water-source heat pump systems
  • Open Loop Systems: Systems using groundwater as heat source
  • Hybrid Geothermal: Combined geothermal and air-source systems

Water-Source Heat Pumps

  • Lake Source Systems: Systems using nearby lakes for heat exchange
  • Well Water Systems: Systems utilizing well water for heating and cooling
  • Closed Loop Water Systems: Recirculating water systems
  • Surface Water Systems: Systems using surface water bodies

Specialty Heat Pump Systems

  • Heat Pump Water Heaters: Efficient water heating systems
  • Pool Heat Pumps: Dedicated systems for pool and spa heating
  • Commercial Heat Pumps: Large-scale systems for commercial applications
  • Absorption Heat Pumps: Gas-powered heat pump systems
