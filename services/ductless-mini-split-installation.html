Why Choose Ductless Mini Split Systems?

Ductless mini split systems have become the preferred choice for Cedarburg homeowners seeking energy-efficient, versatile climate control solutions. These advanced systems offer numerous advantages over traditional HVAC systems:

  • Superior Energy Efficiency: Ductless systems can reduce energy consumption by up to 40% compared to traditional central air systems, thanks to their inverter technology and elimination of duct losses.
  • Individual Zone Control: Each indoor unit operates independently, allowing you to customize temperatures in different rooms according to individual preferences and usage patterns.
  • Easy Installation Process: Without the need for extensive ductwork, installation typically takes just one day with minimal disruption to your daily routine.
  • Enhanced Indoor Air Quality: Multi-stage filtration systems capture allergens, dust, and other pollutants, providing cleaner, healthier indoor air.
  • Whisper-Quiet Operation: Modern ductless systems operate at noise levels as low as 19 decibels, ensuring peaceful comfort.
  • Year-Round Comfort: Heat pump technology provides both efficient cooling and heating, perfect for Wisconsin's diverse climate.

Types of Ductless Mini Split Systems We Install

We offer installation services for all major ductless mini split brands and configurations:

Single-Zone Systems

Perfect for individual rooms, additions, or targeted comfort zones. These systems feature one outdoor unit connected to one indoor unit, providing precise temperature control for specific areas.

Multi-Zone Systems

Ideal for whole-home comfort, multi-zone systems connect one outdoor unit to multiple indoor units (up to 8 zones), each with independent temperature control.

Wall-Mounted Units

The most popular option, wall-mounted units offer excellent performance and easy maintenance access while blending seamlessly with your home's d√©cor.

Ceiling Cassette Units

Recessed into the ceiling, cassette units provide 360-degree airflow distribution and a virtually invisible appearance.

Floor-Mounted Units

Perfect for homes with limited wall space or specific installation requirements, floor-mounted units deliver effective heating and cooling at ground level.

Energy Efficiency and Cost Savings

Wisconsin homeowners consistently report significant energy savings after ductless mini split installation. The advanced inverter technology allows the system to modulate compressor speed based on demand, maintaining consistent temperatures while using minimal energy.

Key efficiency benefits include:

  • SEER ratings up to 30+ for cooling efficiency
  • HSPF ratings up to 13+ for heating efficiency
  • Variable-speed operation reduces energy waste
  • Elimination of duct losses (typically 20-30% of energy)
  • Precise temperature control reduces over-conditioning
  • Smart scheduling and remote control capabilities

Installation Considerations for Wisconsin Climate

Our installation services are specifically tailored for Wisconsin's challenging climate conditions:

Cold-Climate Heat Pumps

Proper Insulation and Sealing

Wisconsin's extreme temperature variations require proper insulation of refrigerant lines and careful sealing of all penetrations to prevent energy loss and moisture issues.

Snow and Ice Protection

Our outdoor unit installations include proper elevation and positioning to prevent snow accumulation and ice formation that could impact system performance.

Drainage Considerations
