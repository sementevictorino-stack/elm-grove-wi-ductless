Common Mini Split Problems and Solutions

Understanding common mini split problems can help Boise homeowners identify issues early and prevent costly repairs. Refrigerant leaks are among the most serious problems, often caused by vibration, corrosion, or improper installation. Signs include reduced cooling capacity, ice formation on indoor units, and unusual hissing sounds. Our technicians use electronic leak detectors to locate leaks quickly and perform EPA-certified repairs to restore proper refrigerant levels.

Electrical problems can manifest as units that won't turn on, intermittent operation, or error codes on display panels. These issues may result from loose connections, damaged wiring, or component failures. Our electrically licensed technicians safely diagnose and repair electrical problems, ensuring your system operates safely and reliably.

Drainage issues are particularly common during Boise's wet spring months when condensate production increases. Clogged drain lines can cause water damage and create conditions for mold growth. We clear blocked drains and install drain safety switches to prevent future water damage. Our preventive maintenance services include regular drain line cleaning to prevent these issues.

Diagnostic Process and Repair Approach

Our systematic diagnostic process ensures accurate problem identification and effective repairs. Refrigerant Services and Environmental Compliance
